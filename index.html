About Sundaw Lawn Care

Sundaw Lawn Care LLC is a small, locally owned lawn care business based in Northeastern Pennsylvania. We focus on simple, dependable services that keep your yard neat, safe, and presentable all season long.

Our approach

We believe in clear communication, reliable scheduling, and consistent quality. Whether it’s a one-time cleanup or recurring mowing, you’ll always know what to expect and when we’ll be there.

Every property is different, so we take the time to understand what you want: shorter or longer grass, attention to edges, or specific areas to avoid. You get a tailored service without the hassle.

Who we serve

We primarily serve homeowners and small properties in and around Lehman, PA. If you are nearby and not sure if you’re in our service area, reach out and we’ll let you know if we can help.

Our goal is to build long-term relationships with clients who want their lawns taken care of so they can focus on everything else in life.

Services

We offer straightforward lawn care services designed to keep your yard looking clean, healthy, and well-maintained. Below are some of the core services currently available.

Regular Lawn Mowing

Scheduled mowing to keep your lawn at a consistent, healthy height during the growing season.

Trimming & Edging

Clean edges around sidewalks, driveways, and obstacles for a sharp, finished appearance.

Yard Cleanup

Light debris pickup, small branches, and general yard tidying as part of your service visit.

Seasonal Services

Spring and fall cleanups, plus other seasonal lawn maintenance options as availability allows.
